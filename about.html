About Us

Tech Optimum is a non-profit organization ran by students. We are an organization dedicated for people who love computer science, engineering, and more!

We use our skills to build off each other to become better programmers. Our goal is to create a community in which people can feel open to ask help on programming related topics. We encourage beginners, intermediate, and advanced coders, all are welcome! We will host a meeting once every 2 weeks for our members.
